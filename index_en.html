The neighbourhood

The flat is situated in the so-called Latin Quarter in Nørrebro between Sankt Hans Square and Nørrebrogade.

The neighbourhood is known for its relaxing and vibrant atmosphere small, creative boutiques, bars, take away’s and cafés.

You’ll be living within five minutes walk of the local cinema Empire Bio close to the Michelin-starred Thai restaurant Kiin Kiin.

You can eat the 8 best pizza in the world at Bæst – an organic, Italian-inspired restaurant with the adjoining bakery Mirabelle – they are also known for their freshly homemade mozzarella.

You can go for a run around the lakes or a stroll in the beautiful Assistens Cemetery. Grab a cold beer in the sun at hip local brewpub BRUS or a delicious homemade ice-cream at Nicecream.

You can shop in lively, cosy, hip Elmegade just one minute from the flat or go to vibrant, colourful and casual Jægersborggade. Take a walk to Copenhagen’s popular market place Torvehallerne or enjoy a beautiful bike ride through the largest park in Copenhagen, Fælledparken – all the way to the beach at Svanemøllen.

Everything is within reach.

The apartment

The flat is a large, bright, beautiful apartment of 112 sq. in a quiet street with a few small cafes and local shops. The flat is on a tall third floor with no elevator.

Enter the large kitchen and make a fresh cup of tea with boiling hot water directly from the tap.

From the kitchen and the yoga room/office two great balconies overlook the ancient, quiet, wilderness of the old Mosaic Cemetery. Sit down. Enjoy the tranquilizing view.

Watch Netflix or HBO series in the large sofa in the living room.

Sleep tight in the large, cool bedroom in the king size quality bed.

Meditate or do yoga in the calm yoga room or sit down with your computer at the desk.

The flat is comfortable for two people without pets or children.

Getting there

The flat is easily found. If you are arriving from the airport there is an excellent metro system that takes you straight to Nørreport in just 15 minutes. From here you can jump on the bus 5A or 350S and get of less than five minutes later in Elmegade (DKkr 36,-/€ 4,86). From where it is just a two-minute walk. Or you can just walk 15 minutes from Nørreport.
